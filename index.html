John Fry

From joining the United States Marine Corps, to serving his local communities for 12 years as a Pre-Hospital Emergency Medical Technician, John Fry takes his work and career seriously. During his time as an EMT, he rose above his peers to become a Field Training Officer after the first year, for the duration of his career. Since leaving Emergency Medical Services, he became one of the top salesman at different Volkswagen dealerships in the region. John is now the founder and president of a small local Baltimore business, Dead Low Industries, LLC where he is launching a podcast about the therapeutic experiences gained from all forms of car building. He takes his experiences with car building, tuning, racing and life experience and gives back to the car community. He is currently building a Volkswagen Golf to be able to race on tracks. He lives in Baltimore with his wife and two children.
